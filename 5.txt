Mars
the fourth in distance from the Sun and the seventh largest planet of the Solar System;
The mass of the planet is 10.7% of the Earth's mass.
Named after Mars, the ancient Roman god of war, corresponding to the ancient Greek Ares.
Sometimes Mars is called a "red planet" because of the reddish shade of the surface imparted to it by iron oxide.
